The Person

Ingrid Bergman

Founder of Ingrid Bergman Interiors

Ingrid Bergman embarked on her interior design journey in 2013 after spending two decades in the high-end fashion industry. Her time at a renowned interior design firm in Dubai provided her with invaluable experience, allowing her to master the nuances of the trade.

Ingrid's extensive background in retail has proven to be a tremendous asset for her clients. This experience has sharpened her impeccable taste in colors and fabrics and honed her ability to source the most exclusive furniture for her agency.

One of Ingrid's most remarkable qualities is her ability to genuinely listen to her clients, ensuring she delivers the beautiful results they envision. She believes this skill is crucial in building strong client relationships, which lead to inspired, creative design solutions and ultimate client satisfaction.

Ingrid's work is global, with a diverse portfolio of projects. Her home in Portugal, complete with a breathtaking garden, has been transformed into an exclusive showroom where guests can experience the essence of luxury living.

Clients are invited to visit the Villa in the Algarve to explore her collections and discuss their projects in this unique and inspiring setting.
